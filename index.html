About Me



My name is Robert Ruelas. I am a Technical Consultant and Software Developer that can help troubleshoot

My objective is to obtain a challenging and rewarding position in the technical industry, leveraging my exceptional
hardware and software skills, strong problem solving, and leadership experience to provide outstanding results that will
contribute to the success of your team.
